Florbs

The Florbs are floor monsters that live under the bed. They are not scary monsters but wacky monsters who love small cute kids feet. Sometimes they can bite into a toe, so do not stand on them for too long at a time. They are not so fond of sour socks and dirty nails.

Florbs

Each Florb has its own personality and characteristics. One loves to twist, one can only go backwards, one is super fast and one loves to spin. But common to them all is that they all love to play and make crazy moves.

Vegan

Vegan fabrics represent the future. They're versatile and environmentally sound and don't involve cruelty to animals—which is increasingly viewed as unjustifiable.

Produced in Denmark

The Florbs are designed and made in Denmark with a focus on sustainability. Produced by wind power and made with up to 60% recycled material. The materials used for the Florbs are TÜV-approved as hypoallergenic and are fragrance-free and antistatic.

PVC free

Proven through years of experience in fitness

Florb inspires movement and makes sure there's a good grip on the floor during activity. The compressed and robust fleece bottom gives the best possible sliding effect and a great amount of friction. The Florb is silent and doesn't leave scratches on the floors. Florbs are not meant for asphalt, carpets or raw grass.

Invites activity

Florbs are not meant to be put away, they are supposed to lay on the floor and welcome people to try them out. There is a selected range of games to try out in the Florb-world section of the page.
